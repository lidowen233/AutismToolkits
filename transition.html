🌿 Zone — Transition

Transition

Mixed textures, layered planting with gradual sensory stimulation, water drill along the path, stepping elements with gentle slopes, soft interactive sounds, dynamic dappled light, and shifting colours — a carefully choreographed sequence that guides the body from one state to another.

Design Strategies
6 Strategies
Texture
Mixed Textures
A gradual shift from smooth to varied surfaces signals movement between zones — preparing the sensory system for what comes next.
Water
Water Drill Along the Path
Linear water features follow path edges, providing a continuous sensory thread that guides movement through transitional space.
Light
Dynamic Dappled Light
Sparsed tree canopy and pergola structures create gently shifting light — bridging the transition between enclosed shelter and open space.
Colour
Transit Colour
Colour gradually shifts along the path — acting as a visual gradient that prepares children for the sensory character of the next zone.
Topography
Gentle Slopes & Stepping Elements
Subtle grade changes and stepping stones invite mindful movement — slowing the body and creating a moment of physical transition.
Sound
Soft Interactive Sounds
Wind sculptures, water channels and rustling plants create layered, gentle acoustics that ease the shift between contrasting sensory environments.

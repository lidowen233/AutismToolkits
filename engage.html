⚡ Zone — Engage

Engage

Rough and tactile surfaces, diverse and open planting, water play and waterfalls, varied topography, interactive and playful sounds, open sunlight, and vivid high-contrast colour — a full sensory landscape that invites movement, discovery, and joyful connection.

Design Strategies
6 Strategies
Texture
Rough / Tactile Texture
Bark, rock, woven surfaces and uneven ground invite touch and proprioceptive input — stimulating the body and encouraging active exploration.
Water
Water Play
Water fall features, splash pads and interactive jets provide rich tactile, auditory, and vestibular stimulation for full-body engagement.
Light
Open Sunlight
Sparse canopy and open planting allow direct sunlight — energising the space and supporting active movement and play.
Colour
Vivid & High Contrast Colour
Bold, saturated tones across equipment, signage and planting create visual excitement and draw children into the space.
Topography
Varied Topography
Hills, slopes, and undulating ground challenge balance and proprioception — making movement itself a sensory experience.
Sound
Interactive & Playful Sounds
Water splash, musical play equipment and group play noise create a lively acoustic environment that energises and rewards participation.
